Announcement of Program Availability

The Conflict Research Consortium is pleased to announce publication, over the World Wide Web, of its International Online Training Program On Intractable Conflict.  This major new resource provides education or professional training in the fields of peace and conflict. as well as an online consulting service for people seeking quick access to the field's knowledge base.  Program provides free and instantaneous access to full-text   materials outlining the full range of concepts related to the conflict resolution and peace research fields. There are no expensive texts to buy; all that you need is   access to the World Wide Web.
