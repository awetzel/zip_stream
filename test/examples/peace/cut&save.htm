Understanding the Distinction Between Manifest and Latent Conflict
Manifest conflicts involve situations in which the parties are actively engaged in an ongoing confrontation with one another.  In latent conflicts there are underlying tensions and disagreements which have not yet erupted into an active conflict even though they clearly have the potential to do so.
