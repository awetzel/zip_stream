International Online Training Program on Intractable Conflict

of
Conflict Management and Constructive Confrontation
Are you involved in a conflict you do not know how to handle or one that never seems to end?
Are you trying to help others who are involved in a difficult or long-lasting conflict?
Are you interested in learning about more constructive ways of approaching difficult conflicts in general?
Are you interested in learning more about the field of conflict resolution?

If you answered "yes" to any of these questions, this online training program is designed to help you.
