USER SURVEY - REQUEST FOR COMMENTS
Please fill out our user survey and give us comments again after you have used the system--tell us what you like, what you do not like, and how we can make the system better for you.  E-mail links which you can use to contact us with your comments are found at the bottom of almost every page.
 
HOW TO USE THE SYSTEM
Information on how the system is organized and different options for moving around in it. 
 
USING THE SYSTEM ON YOUR OWN

ONLINE TRAINING: USING THE SYSTEM AS A FORMAL COURSE
Information about taking the course for university credit or for a certificate of completion (without credit, but with instructor feedback). Shortcut to class units and assignments.
