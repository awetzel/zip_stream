List of  Solution (Treatment) Categories

Note:  Each of these topics links to a list of potential treatments in that category.  That list then links to the full write-ups.

  • Framing  - defining problems in a constructive way
  • Scoping - determining the extent of and context of a conflict
  • Communication - ways to improve communication between the parties
  • Fact-Finding - ways to agree on contested facts
  • Procedural Issues - ways to agree on fair and efficient decision making procedures
  • Escalation - ways to limit escalation
  • Force - strategies to use force effectively and legitimately
  • Integrative System - strategies to bring people back together, to improve relationships, trust, and sense of community
  • Exchange - ways to negotiate effectively
  • Combination Approaches - approaches which combine one or more of the above categories
